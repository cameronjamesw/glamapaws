Golden Doodle

About Me!

Not considered an actual breed of dog, it is in fact a cross of two breeds – this is why it isn’t registered through the Kennel Club. It is a hybrid of the Poodle and the Golden Retriever. Known for it’s curly yet golden coat. Found to be successful as service and therapy dogs.

Personality

  • A very gentle and friendly dog, they thrive with peaceful and calm families. They rarely bark due to their nature.
  • Very friendly and intelligent dogs meaning they are trainable. Coming from a poodle and a golden retriever, the golden doodle is very loyal and devoted just like both of its parents.
  • They are not sensitive to noisy environments – meaning they are exceptional with children.

Health

  • They will require occasional visits to the vets for health checks and other vaccinations and boosters from time to time.
  • They have a life expectancy of around 12-15 years.

Exercise

  • High athletic dogs that require a great deal of physical stimulation to prevent them from partaking in more destructive activities around the house.
  • Around 30 minutes of exercise a day will be more than enough for them.

Maintenance

  • They will need their coat brushed regularly as well as having their nails clipped if necessary.
  • They require baths, when necessary, in the event that they’re extremely dirty from getting into mischief. This will also help prevent skin diseases, and also help get rid of ticks and flees.
