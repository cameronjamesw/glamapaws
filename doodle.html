Golden Doodle

About Me!

I’m not considered an actual breed of dog, I am in-fact a cross of two breeds – this is why I’m not registered through the Kennel Club. I am a hybrid of the Poodle and the Golden Retriever, and I’m known for my curly yet golden coat.

Personality

  • I am a very gentle and friendly dog, I thrive with peaceful and calm families. I rarely bark due to my calm, relaxed nature.
  • I’m very friendly and intelligent, and with enough patience I am easily trainable. Coming from a Poodle and a Golden Retriever, I am a very loyal and devoted breed, just like both of my parents.
  • I’m not very sensitive to noisy environments – meaning I’m exceptional with children.

Health

  • I will require occasional visits to the vets for health checks and other vaccinations and boosters for me from time to time.
  • I have a life expectancy of around 12-15 years.

Exercise

  • I am a highly athletic breed of dog that requires a great deal of physical stimulation to prevent me from partaking in more destructive activities around the house.
  • Around 30 minutes of exercise a day will be more than enough for me!

Maintenance

  • I need my coat brushed regularly as well as having my nails clipped if necessary.
  • I require a bath whenever necessary, only if I’m extremely dirty from getting into mischief. This will also help protect me from skin diseases, and also help get rid of ticks and flees.

Overall, I am a very easy-going dog that doesn’t ask for much maintenance or exercise at all. All I ask for is a loving family who are prepared to incorporate me into all of their fun family activities!
