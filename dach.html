Miniature Dachshund

About Me

I am a very versatile family dog – I’m originally from Germany, and I’m known for my long body and short legs.


There are 3 variations of the Dachshunds – short fur, long fur, wire-fur o Short fur Dachshunds do not require much attention towards their coats, however because they are so short it does mean they will need a coat within the colder months. o Long-haired Dachshunds need their coat groomed daily to prevent matting of fur.

Personality

  • I am a loyal, joyful dog who can be very independent due to my instinct to hunt.
  • I’m entertaining and affectionate – a very cuddly dog that enjoys cuddling up on the sofa!
  • I’m intelligent enough to learn commands and tricks with a bit of patience.
  • I create a strong bond with one certain person within the family – this doesn’t add any limitations for the rest of the family though!

Health

  • I am considered a healthy breed – however I can be prone to certain health problems:
    • IDD (Invertibral Disk Disease)
    • Epilepsy
    • Deafness
  • Be cautious when letting me jump off your sofa, or other elevated furniture, due to my unusual body shape, this can create issues for me if I jump from too high up.
  • I have a life expectancy of 12-16 years.

Exercise

  • Due to my nature, I can be very energetic so lots of exercise and walking is heavily advised! I also enjoy digging and hunting when out and about!
  • I can be prone to obesity, so it is very important that I get enough exercise!
  • I’m very stubborn at times; however, I am very good learning tricks and commands.

Maintenance

  • I do not need to be bathed too often, only when necessary – for example if I have gotten muddy after a walk in the rain!
  • Grooming is necessary for the wire-coat and long-coat Dachshund, but not too often for the short-coat variation.

Overall, we are very loving, family-friendly dogs that can adapt to almost any household! It’s best to be aware of the health issues that we may encounter, but with the right precautions they won’t be an issue!
