Miniature Dachshund

About Me

Very versatile family dogs – originally from Germany, the dog is known for hunting badgers, rabbits and other small rodents. Easily recognisable with their long bodies and short legs. There are 3 variarions of Dachshund; short-coat, long-coat and wire-coat. At Glamapaws we only breed the short-coat and long-coat variations!

Personality

  • Loyal, joyful dogs who can be very independent due to their instinct to hunt.
  • Entertaining and affectionate – very cuddly dogs that enjoy cuddling up on the sofa!
  • Intelligent enough to learn commands and tricks.
  • They create a strong bond with one certain person within the family – this fortunately doesn’t add any limitations for the rest of the family though!

Health

  • Considered a healthy breed – however they can be prone to certain health problems.
    • IDD (Invertibral Disk Disease)
    • Epilepsy
    • Deafness
  • Be cautious when letting your Dachshund jump on and off your sofa, due to their unusual body shape, this can create issues for them if they jump from too high up.
  • Have a life expectancy of 12-16 years.

Exercise

  • Due to their nature, they can be very energetic so lots of exercise and walking is heavily advised! They also enjoy digging and hunting when out and about!
  • They can be prone to obesity, so it is very important that they get enough exercise!
  • They can be very stubborn at times; however, they are very good learning tricks and commands.

Maintenance

  • These dogs do not need to be bathed too often, only when necessary – for example if they have gotten muddy after a walk in the rain!
  • Grooming is necessary for the wire-coat and long-coat Dachshund, but not too often for the short-coat variation.

Overall, Dachshunds are very loving, family-friendly dogs that can adapt to almost any household! It’s best to be aware of the health issues they can encounter, but with the right precautions they won’t be an issue!
