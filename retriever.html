Golden Retriever

About Me!

I am originally a gun dog for Scotland. I make a great service dog through serving for the special forces and people with disabilities.

Personality

  • I am one of the most intelligent and affectionate dog breeds.
  • I have a strong willingness to learn, explore and be trained.
  • I prefer wide open spaces where I can run around freely – if I am kept cooped up without attention then I will release my energy through more destructive methods.
  • Regular training and attention is required to mentally and physically stimulate me!

Health

  • I am a muscular and medium sized dog.
  • I have a life expectancy of around 12 years.
  • I require regular exercise, around 30 minutes per day. Without regular exercise, I can become sad, destructive, and aggressive.
  • An occasional visit to the vets will be required as I will require vaccines and boosters from time to time!

Temperament

  • I am obedient and I have a strong sense of purpose within family life – I can fit into almost any family environment!
  • I make a great therapy dog as I have such a strong sense of emotional understanding of those around me.
  • I am great with children, and studies have shown I can actually aid toddlers with my nature through helping them learn to walk and crawl. Due to my sense of purpose, I am very protective around children.

Maintenance

  • I require regular baths to protect against ticks, flees and skin diseases I could run into whilst exploring.
  • I do not need to be groomed too regularly as I have a shedding coat, however brushing me twice a week will be a benefit to me, too!

I am a very affectionate and loving family-orientated dog who can fit into almost any family environment! I require a fair amount of exercise and training in order to keep me happy, and if that’s not too much to ask then maybe I’m the dog for you!
